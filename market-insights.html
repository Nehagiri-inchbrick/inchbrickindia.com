Expert Opinions

Views from Inchbrick advisors, industry analysts, and partner economists on where the market is headed.

The NCR expressway corridor is entering a price discovery phase. Buyers who enter before Jewar airport commercial operations will likely see disproportionate appreciation over a 3–5 year hold.

NCR Outlook

Priya Sharma

Senior Investment Advisor, Inchbrick

Bangalore's rental market remains the strongest in India for mid-segment apartments. Investors should prioritise projects within 2 km of metro stations — tenant demand is structurally higher there.

Rental Strategy

Rahul Mehta

Head of Research, Inchbrick

NRIs are returning to Indian real estate with renewed confidence. Dubai and US-based buyers are especially active in Hyderabad and Pune, favouring ready-to-move gated communities with rental management.

NRI Demand

Ananya Desai

NRI Advisory Lead, Inchbrick

Commercial real estate is bifurcating — Grade A offices in CBDs are fully recovered, while secondary locations face vacancy pressure. Residential investors should watch office absorption as a leading indicator.

Commercial Trends

Vikram Singh

Commercial Markets Analyst

Affordable housing under ₹ 45 lakh still has the strongest end-user pull. However, investors should be cautious — capital appreciation in this segment is modest compared to mid-premium corridors.

Affordable Segment
