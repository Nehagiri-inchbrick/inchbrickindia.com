About Mumbai

Mumbai is India's financial capital and one of the most dynamic real estate markets in the country. From sea-facing luxury towers in Worli and Bandra to suburban townships in Thane and Navi Mumbai, the city offers diverse options for end-users and investors.

  • Strong rental demand in western suburbs
  • Metro expansion improving connectivity
  • Premium resale liquidity in island city belts
  • NRI-friendly documentation support

FAQ

Is Mumbai good for property investment in 2026?

Select micro-markets with metro connectivity and established developer track records continue to show steady appreciation and rental demand.

What is the average price per sq.ft in Mumbai?

Prices vary widely — from ₹ 12,000/sqft in peripheral suburbs to ₹ 50,000+/sqft in premium west-side locations.

Are RERA projects mandatory in Maharashtra?

Yes. Always verify the RERA registration number on the MahaRERA portal before booking.
