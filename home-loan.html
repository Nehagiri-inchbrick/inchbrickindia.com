Criteria

Eligibility

  • Age: 21–65 years (at loan maturity)
  • Minimum income: ₹ 25,000/month (salaried) or ₹ 3 Lakh p.a. (self-employed)
  • Credit score: 750+ preferred for best rates
  • Employment: Salaried (2+ years) or self-employed (3+ years in business)
  • Loan-to-value: Up to 80–90% of property value
  • Co-applicant income can be clubbed to improve eligibility
Paperwork

Required Documents

Identity & Address

  • PAN Card
  • Aadhaar / Passport / Voter ID
  • Address proof (utility bill / rent agreement)

Income Proof

  • Last 3 months salary slips
  • 6 months bank statements
  • Form 16 / ITR (2 years)
  • Business proof & GST (if self-employed)

Property Documents

  • Sale agreement / allotment letter
  • Approved building plan & OC/CC
  • Title chain & encumbrance certificate
