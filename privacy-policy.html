Privacy Policy

This policy explains how Inchbrick Realty collects, uses, and safeguards your information when you use our website and property advisory services.

Last updated: May 22, 2026

1. Introduction

Inchbrick Realty ("we", "us", "our") operates inchbrickrealty.com and related services. By accessing our website, submitting enquiries, or using our advisory services, you agree to the practices described in this Privacy Policy. If you do not agree, please discontinue use of our services.

2. Information we collect

We may collect the following types of information:

  • Contact details: name, phone number, email address, and city of interest.
  • Property preferences: budget, property type, location, and investment goals you share with us.
  • Technical data: IP address, browser type, device information, and cookies when you browse our site.
  • Communication records: messages, callback requests, and site visit bookings.

3. How we use your information

We use your information to:

  • Respond to enquiries and provide property recommendations.
  • Schedule site visits and coordinate with builders or partners on your behalf.
  • Send updates about projects, offers, or services you have opted in to receive.
  • Improve our website, user experience, and marketing effectiveness.
  • Comply with applicable laws, including anti-fraud and record-keeping requirements.

4. Sharing of information

We do not sell your personal data. We may share information with trusted builders, channel partners, home loan providers, or legal consultants only when necessary to fulfil your request and with your consent. We may also disclose information if required by law or to protect our rights and users' safety.

5. Cookies & analytics

Our website may use cookies and similar technologies to remember preferences, analyse traffic, and measure campaign performance. You can control cookies through your browser settings; disabling them may affect certain site features.

6. Data security

We implement reasonable administrative, technical, and physical safeguards to protect your information. However, no method of transmission over the internet is completely secure, and we cannot guarantee absolute security.

7. Your rights

Subject to applicable Indian law, you may request access to, correction of, or deletion of your personal data. You may also opt out of marketing communications at any time by contacting us or using unsubscribe links in our emails.

8. Data retention

We retain personal information only as long as needed to provide services, meet legal obligations, or resolve disputes. When no longer required, data is securely deleted or anonymised.

9. Third-party links

Our website may contain links to third-party sites (e.g. builder websites, payment portals). We are not responsible for the privacy practices of those sites and encourage you to review their policies separately.
